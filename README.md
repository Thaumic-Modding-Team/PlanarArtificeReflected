# Cursed Artifice
Planar Artifice rewrite.


## New Content
- Enchanted Thaumaturge's Fruit - An upgraded Enchanted Golden Apple.
- Flawless Magic Mirror - Magic Mirror with significantly reduce flux pollution and faster transfer speed.
- Flawless Essentia Mirror - Essentia Mirror with significantly reduce flux pollution.
- Mirroed Infusion Enchant - Link a weapon or tool to a Magic Mirror to instantly transport any dropped items to that mirror.

## Overhauled Content
- Alkimium Essentia Centrifuge - Rewritten. Significantly faster than the Essentia Centrifuge, Primal Aspects pass through the centrifuge without jamming the system, and centrifuge converts and transfers all essentia obtained during the breakdown.
- Alkimium Goggles - Rewritten. Provides the same information as the Essentia Resonator while looking at pipes or machinery.
- Alkimium Essentia Smelters - Rewritten. Significantly less inconsistent behavior with these blocks.
- Alkimium Vent/Slurry Pump - Rewritten. Smelter addons now work with Thaumcraft and Planar Artifice Essentia Smelters.
- Belt of Suspension - Rewritten. Can now be used to hover by holding jump or float downwards by crouching.
- Bismuth Caster's Gauntlet - Rewritten. Drains vis from the 5x5 chunk area surrounding the player at the cost of 20% increased spell cost. Can receive up to 4 - Glass Cutters - Rewritten. Now instantly harvest most glass blocks on interact.
- Mirrored Amulet - Rewritten. While worn, transfers any items dropped on death to a linked Magic Mirror.
augments if Thaumic Augmentation is installed.

## Cut Content
- Alchemical Scribing Tools - Removed. Original item was broken. Use New Crimson Revelations for a similar feature.
- Bismuth Claymore - Removed. Extremely overpowered weapon.
- Bush Alchemy - Pruned. Removed duplicate recipes between Thaumcraft addons. Use Thaumic Wonders Unofficial for similar features.
- Condensed Crystal Cluster - Removed. No real use outside of turning Primal Essentia into other Primal Essentia.
- Curious Infusion Enchant - Removed. Original enchant hand an extremely low chance of occurring. Use ThaumicTweaker's curiosity drops tweak for a similar feature.
- Dimensional Curiosity - Removed. Planar research was removed.
- Focus Effect: Prismatic Light - Removed. Original spell was broken and caused issues on servers. Use New Crimson Revelations for a similar feature.
- Flux Scrubber - Removed. Original block was extremely overpowered. No replacement.
- Fundamental Curiosity - Removed. No replacement.
- Mirromirous Matrix - Removed. Teleportation was limited and inconsistent. Use Thaumic Wonders Unofficial for a similar feature.
- Projecting Infusion Enchant - Removed. Original enchant was bugged and would increase reach distance by up to +10 blocks at the cost of all attack speed.
- Transmutative Infusion Enchant - Removed. Recipes were extremely limited and required significant integrations to be useful.
